Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SimplePDF?

SimplePDF is a web-based PDF generator that allows users to create PDFs from typed text, uploaded text files, and images. It provides a fast, secure, and user-friendly way to generate documents for personal or educational use.

2. How do I generate a PDF?

To generate a PDF, simply type your text in the input box or upload files/images. Click the "Generate PDF" button, and after a short processing time, the download button will appear to save your PDF locally.

3. Can I upload multiple files?

Yes, you can upload multiple text files and images simultaneously. The files will be processed in the order you select them, and all content will appear in the generated PDF.

4. Is my uploaded data saved on a server?

No. Uploaded files and typed text are processed temporarily in memory. SimplePDF does not store your files permanently, ensuring privacy and security.

5. Can I use SimplePDF offline?

SimplePDF requires an internet connection for loading the web app and any online resources. The processing of files happens in your browser, but the app itself is online.

6. Are there ads in the app?

The app may include banner placeholders for future AdSense integration. Currently, the focus is on providing a free, functional PDF generator without distractions.

7. Can I customize the theme?

Yes. There is a toggle button to switch between dark and light themes. The default theme is dark for a futuristic look.

8. Is SimplePDF free to use?

Yes. All basic functionalities, including text input and file uploads for PDF generation, are completely free. No subscriptions or payments are required at this stage.

9. How do I accept cookies?

A cookie banner appears at the bottom of the screen when you first visit. Click "Accept" to enable cookies, which allows the app to remember your preferences and improve performance.

10. What if I encounter issues?

For technical issues or questions, contact us via email at vofs.auto@gmail.com. We aim to respond promptly to all inquiries.

11. Can I use SimplePDF for commercial purposes?

You may use generated PDFs for personal or commercial purposes, provided that your usage complies with copyright and intellectual property laws. Do not use SimplePDF to distribute illegal or harmful content.

12. Does SimplePDF work on mobile devices?

Yes. SimplePDF is fully responsive and works on Android, iOS, and desktop browsers. The interface adjusts automatically to screen size for optimal usability.

13. Is there a limit to the number of PDFs I can generate?

Currently, there is no enforced limit. Users can generate as many PDFs as needed, but extremely large files or images may affect performance.

14. Can I save my generated PDFs locally?

Yes. Once a PDF is generated, a "Download PDF" button appears. Clicking it allows you to save the PDF directly to your device.

15. How do I contact SimplePDF support?

All support inquiries should be sent to vofs.auto@gmail.com. We provide assistance with usage questions, bug reports, and feedback to improve the app.
